About

About TapCart

TapCart is a prototype for a phone-based self-scan shopping experience.

📱 Mobile
📷 Easy QRs
🛒 Shop fast
ℹ️ Extra info

Scan product QR codes, build your cart while you shop, and generate a checkout QR code at the end, without having to use hand-held scanners or waiting endlessly in a queue.

How it works

Scan products

Find QR codes on products you wish to add to your cart and use your phone camera to scan (by granting permission for camera access).

Build your cart

See your scanned items, quantities, and running total before checkout, and make any adjustments necessary.

View your products

Click on any product to see additional details and information.

Use the menu

Use the menu to access additional options such as language changes or beta-functionality.

Generate checkout QR

Generate a QR code representing your cart for final payment by clicking the checkout button.

Terms and conditions

This prototype does not process payments, validate purchases, manage inventory, or connect to a secure backend. It should not be used as a production shopping system and is only meant to be used as prototype.
